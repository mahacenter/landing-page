About us

MAP 4i.

MAP 4i - Mapping & Analytics Platform for Immunization - is an international not-for-profit organization, practically embedded into a digital platform, which provides immunizations related information management services to development organizations, enabling partners to make informed decisions towards Zero-Dose Children – or not vaccinated- based strategy.

All the running expenses of the digital platform such as the hosting of the servers, any further developpment/improvement and related expenses of the Zero-Dose Children application are allocated to MAP 4I.

For additional Technical Assistance, the digital platform MAP 4i has a complementary partner Mapping & Analytics for Health Activities (MAHA)…

MAHA.

The digital platform has a complemenray partner Mapping & Analytics for Health Activities (MAHA)…that ultimately provides high-quality targeted Technical Assistance (TA) to the partnered organizations; the TA services refer mainly to the trainings about the functionalities of the Geographical Information System (GIS) aplications and the setting up and maintenance of server hosting.

Reminder: The Zero-Dose Children platform has been configured to be easily scalable, thanks to a powerful coding approach and outsourced database/server solutions.

There are two options: Use our cloud architecture or deploy onto local servers.

Partnership.

MAP 4i & MAHA support both international funding and in-country implementing organizations alike, to implement operational and strategic Geographical Information System (GIS) solutions about Expanded Programme on Immunizations (EPI) challenges.

MAP 4i can receive financial contributions from international funding and private sector based organizations alike.
