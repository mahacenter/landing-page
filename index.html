About us

MAP 4i.

MAP 4i - Mapping & Analytics Platform for Immunization - is an international not-for-profit organization, which provides Zero-Dose Children related information to in-country & development organizations, thanks to a devoted digital platform.

MAHA.

The digital platform MAP 4i has a complementary partner, namely MAHA (Mapping & Analytics for Health Activities)…that provides high-quality Technical Assistance to the partnered organizations.

Reminder: The Zero-Dose Children platform developed by MAHA has been configured to be easily scalable.

Partnership.

MAP 4i & MAHA support both international funding and in-country implementing organizations alike, to implement operational and strategic Geographical Information System (GIS) solutions about Expanded Programme on Immunizations (EPI) challenges.

MAP 4i can receive financial contributions from international funding and private sector based organizations alike.
